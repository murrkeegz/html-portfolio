The Best Movies According to Mary

My top 3 movies that came out in 2024 (that I actually saw.)

Sasquatch Sunset

A funny, poignant, wordless domestic drama/survival story about the last of an apocrphyal breed of North American primates.

Late Night With The Devil

A horror period piece with a fresh take on found footage and possession narratives, bonus points for a refreshingly original monster design.

Small Things Like These

Heartbreaking film based on the novel, based on real events in Ireland in the early 90s. An emotional slow burn with beautiful cinematography.
